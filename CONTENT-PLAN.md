## Content Templates

### Blog Post Structure:

```markdown
# [Catchy Title with Keyword]

## Introduction (100-150 words)
- Hook the reader
- State the problem/opportunity
- Preview what they'll learn

## [H2 Section 1]
- Main point with examples
- Include keyword naturally

## [H2 Section 2]
- Supporting information
- Data, statistics, or case studies

## [H2 Section 3]
- Practical tips or steps
- Bullet points for readability

## [H2 Section 4]
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Expert insights

## Conclusion (50-100 words)
- Summarize key points
- Call-to-action (contact us, read more, etc.)

## FAQ (Optional)
- 3-5 common questions with answers
```

### Product Page Structure:

